The Visitor Experience

A curated journey across six zones, designed to compress a lifetime of astronaut awe into a single day.

Moon Zone

Walk on an AI-rendered lunar surface, feel 1/6th gravity, and watch Earth rise over the horizon in real time.

  • Physics-accurate low-gravity simulation.
  • Audio-reactive Earthrise moments for maximum awe.
  • Guided missions with AI astronaut companions.

Mars Zone

Navigate red dust terrain, survive dust storms, and drive rovers across a simulated Martian landscape.

  • Terrain generated from NASA Mars elevation data.
  • Dynamic weather: storms, low visibility, mission drills.
  • STEM challenges for schools and families.

ISS Simulator

Live like an astronaut: eat space food, sleep in zero-gravity pods, and conduct microgravity experiments.

  • 1:1 modules modeled after the ISS interior.
  • Hands-on STEM experiments for all ages.
  • Live-link overlays with real ISS telemetry.

Launch Experience

Strap in for a launch sequence that simulates real G‑forces, vibration, and sound — from countdown to orbit.

  • Full-motion seats synced to flight profile.
  • Immersive 360° visuals and spatial audio.
  • Selectable mission profiles (Moon, Mars, LEO).

Deep Space Dome

Stand under a living universe: AI-rendered galaxies, nebulae, and black holes that react to audience emotion.

  • Real-time cosmic visualizations from live data feeds.
  • Emotion-aware sequences tuned for peak awe.
  • Special night sessions for stargazer clubs.

Alien Worlds Biodome

Step into AI-generated alien ecosystems — speculative life forms based on cutting-edge astrobiology.

  • Procedurally generated planets and biomes.
  • Interactive “what if?” scenarios for classrooms.
  • Accessible science storytelling for all ages.
